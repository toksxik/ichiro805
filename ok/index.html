Examples & case studies

Short descriptions of how AI is applied across domains.

Education — Personalized tutoring

Adaptive systems tailor practice sets, spacing and explanations to each student's needs — improving retention and motivation.

  • • Automated practice & feedback
  • • Curriculum personalization
  • • Language support & translation

Healthcare — Decision support

AI helps clinicians analyze images and patient data to flag conditions early and suggest possible next steps.

  • • Faster triage
  • • Assistance with rare conditions
  • • Monitoring and remote care

Productivity — Automating tasks

From email triage to data extraction, AI automates repetitive work so humans can focus on higher-value tasks.

  • • Smart summaries
  • • Auto-generated reports
  • • Scheduling assistance

Creativity — Co-creation tools

AI augments artists, musicians, and writers by generating ideas, variations, or polishing drafts while the human keeps authorship and judgment.

Safety & environment

AI models can help predict natural hazards, optimize energy use, and detect anomalies to reduce environmental impact.
